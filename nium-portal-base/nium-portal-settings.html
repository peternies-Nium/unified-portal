Verification of Payee override

Nium automatically verifies the beneficiary name against the payout instruction. By default, transactions with a name mismatch are blocked to help prevent misdirected payments. You can choose to override this setting and allow such transactions to proceed. This may increase the risk of funds being sent to the wrong party.
